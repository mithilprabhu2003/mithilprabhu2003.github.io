Smart Water Turbidity and Pipeline Management

An IoT-based project designed for real-time monitoring of water quality and pipeline health. The system uses advanced sensors to detect turbidity and pipeline issues, providing timely alerts for efficient and safe water management.

Objectives

  • Monitor water turbidity and pipeline status in real-time.
  • Enable early detection of water quality issues and pipeline faults.

Key Features

  • IoT sensors for continuous data collection.
  • Automated alerts for abnormal turbidity or pipeline conditions.
  • User-friendly dashboard for monitoring and analysis.
